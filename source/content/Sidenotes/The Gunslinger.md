**Ability Scores**: Str, Dex, Int

**Feat**: Savage Attacker

**Skill Proficiency**: Perception or Sleight of Hand

**Tool Proficiencies**: Smith's tools and either Tinker's tools or Thieves' tools

**Equipment**: **THE GUN**, 20 bullets, Smith's tools and either Tinker's tools or Thieves' tools, 4 GP.


One way or another you came across this piece of experimental dwarven tech: **THE GUN** - a unique non-magical (yet) item.

**Tags**: Martial Weapon, Ranged Weapon, Ammunition
**Price**: priceless
**Weight**: 6.5 lb.	
**Damage**: 2d10 Piercing
**Range** 20/60 ft.
**Mastery**: Scatter

**Two-Handed**. It requires two hands when you attack with it.

**Heavy**. You have Disadvantage on attack rolls with it if your Dexterity score isn't at least 13.

**Complex Loading**. You can fire only one piece of ammunition when you use an action, a bonus action, or a reaction to fire it, regardless of the number of attacks you can normally make. You then have to spend an action to reload it.

**Destructible Ammo**. The ammunition cannot be retrieved.

**Recoil**. After you make an attack with this weapon, you can't make ranged attacks beyond the weapon's normal range until the end of the current turn.

**Mastery**: Scatter. Being within 5 feet of an enemy doesn't impose Disadvantage on your ranged attack rolls with this weapon.


You are proficient with **THE GUN**. Furthermore, during your downtime you can utilize Smith's tools and either Tinker's tools or Thieves' tools in order to modify **THE GUN**, tinkering its features to your fighting style. You can improve its stats or add/remove features. Tinkering with **THE GUN** requires resources (like raw ores, junky trinkets or even magical items) and time, which may vary depending on your goal (up to dm perversion). Also, during short or long rests you can utilize Smith's tools and either Tinker's tools or Thieves' tools to craft bullets. For **THE GUN**.
