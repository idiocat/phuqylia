You must choose which mastery you want to use before you make an attack roll. The only exception is Graze.

**Cleave**. Only roll once, apply it to both targets.
*Melee*: the position of the second target relative to the first one is irrelevant.
*Ranged*: the second target must be located within 30 ft behind the first target.

**Concuss**. If you hit a creature with this weapon, it can’t take reactions until the start of its next turn.

**Lunge**. This weapon's range is increased by 5 ft.

**Pull**. If you hit a creature with this weapon, you can pull the creature 5 feet towards you if it is no more than one size larger than you.

**Shift**. If you hit a creature with this weapon, you can move up to 5 ft without provoking opportunity attacks from the creature.

**Switch**. This weapon has a capability of dealing whatever damage type (piercing, slashing, bludgeoning). Choose the damage type when you use this mastery.
