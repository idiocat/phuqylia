**Magic Bullshit** is a buff that you get upon using certain class features. Its effect is determined by randomly choosing from the list below. It lasts until you cast a spell or for 1 minute. If you get this buff again, its duration is refreshed and its effect is replaced.
1) you restore a spellslot;
2) your next spell either gets +1 to its attack roll or save DC;
3) you gain a random spell, that can be cast without expanding a spellslot and without its normal M component;
4) your next spell is upcasted by 1 lvl;
5) your next spell's range either increased by 60 ft. or becomes 30 ft., if its range is touch;
6) your next spell's radius or amount of targets is increased by 5 ft. or 1.
