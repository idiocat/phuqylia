**Fey Affinity**. You have advantage against charm.

**Fey features**. You have glowing eyes, a shimmering that surrounds your silhouette in dim light, etc. (at least one).

**Arcane Inclination**. You know the Prestidigitation cantrip. Whenever you finish a short rest, you can replace that cantrip with a different cantrip from the Wizard spell list. At lvl 3 you learn Detect Magic, Identify and Find Familiar, you can cast each of these spells once without expending a spellslot and M.

**Extensive Study**. You have proficiency in the Arcana, Religion or History skill.
