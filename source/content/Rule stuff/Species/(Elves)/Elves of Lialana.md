**Aberrant Affinity**. You can cast Scrying spell with no components and without using a spellslot. You can do so 3 times.

**Aberrant features**. You appearance features change over time, you may temporarily gain features of other families.

**Ancestral Knowledge**. You can mystically access a reservoir of experiences of your whole species. Whenever you finish a long rest, you gain proficiency in one skill of your choice and with one weapon (including one mastery for it) or tool of your choice. These proficiencies last until the end of your next long rest.

**Interconnected Mind**. You know the mage hand cantrip, the hand is invisible when you cast the cantrip with this trait, and you can control it as a part of any magic action.
Starting at lvl 3, you can cast Comprehend Languages. Starting at lvl 5, you can also cast Detect Thoughts. You can cast each spell once without expanding a spellslot or using any components.

**Psychic Resilience**. You have resistance to psychic damage.
