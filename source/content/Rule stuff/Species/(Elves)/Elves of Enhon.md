**Celestial Affinity**. Instead of rolling for the amount of hp healed, apply the maximum possible. This can be used once per long rest, as a reaction, on a creature that is receiving healing and that you can see within 30 ft.

**Celestial features**. You have a halo.

**Beacon of Light**. Your halo emits light and have the following modes.
	*Power-saving mode*. Emits dim light in 5 feet radius.
	*Performance mode*. As a bonus action you can brighten up your halo, making it emit bright light in 30 ft radius and dim light for an additional 30 ft.
	*Overcharge mode*. As a bonus action, you can overcharge your halo and cause creatures of your choice in 30 feet radius to make a con save (your spellcasting ability for this is intelligence, wisdom or charisma), on a fail they're blinded for a minute. When you do, your halo goes into power-saving mode until the end of a long rest and you cannot use any other mode or Light Manipulation. 

**Light Manipulation**. You know Light and Lightning Lure cantrip. When you cast light on an object, you can use Overcharge mode and Lightning Lure as if you were in place of that object.

**Beacon of Hope**. Whenever you roll initiative, you can choose a creature you can see and that can see you (except yourself), it can add your prof. to its initiative roll. Also, whenever you roll initiative or at the start of your turn, you can swap your initiative with an ally who's lower on the order.

**Hope Manipulation**. You can speak Celestial and one other language of your choice. Also, you gain advantage on checks made to inspire others.
