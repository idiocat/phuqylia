**Primordial Affinity**. Choose a skill you're proficient with. You gain expertise in this skill, and whenever you roll 9 or less on a check, you can treat that roll as 10 (if you already have this benefit from a feature like reliable talent, treat that roll as 15). You can change this skill once a week.

**Primal features**. You have dark skin and white/lightgrey hair.

**Darkness Affinity**. When in dim light or in darkness, you can freely cast invisibility on yourself. When cast this way, its effect ends when you move. You also know the Minor Illusion cantrip. You learn Detect Evil and Good at level 3, and Darkness at level 5, you can cast each of these spells once without expending a spellslot. When you cast these spells using this feature, they can be cast without components and without breaking invisibility.

**Guardian of the Night**. You have proficiency with glaive, scimitar, longsword, longbow and whip, as well as light armour. Also, you have proficiency in stealth.

**Darkvision**! You have darkvision of 120 feet. You can see through magical darkness.

**Photosensitivity**! You have disadvantage on attack rolls and on perception checks that rely on sight when you, the target of your attack, or whatever you are trying to perceive is in direct sunlight. You have disadvantage against blinding. Also, while in direct sunlight you take 1d4 fire damage at the start of your turn, unless your skin is fully covered.
