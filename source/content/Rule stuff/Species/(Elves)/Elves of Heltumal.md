**Demonic Affinity**. You have advantage against fear.

**Demonic features**. You have red skin, demonic eyes (black sclera, reddish or dark iris, vertical pupil) and bodyblades (blade-like bones or blade-sharp claws, etc.).

**Destructive Impulse**. Your blades are considered light finesse weapons and deal d4 damage.
Also, when you attack an object, you can choose to critically succeed; you can use this feature {prof.} times per long rest.

**Self-destructive Impulse**. As a 10 minute ritual, you can consume a bladed magical weapon. The weapon is destroyed and your bodyblades incorporate one of its qualities of your choice (magical or not). The amount of qualities cannot exceed {prof.}. Be informed, that the blade diet may cause side-effects and side cuts.

**Wrong Vibe**. You gain proficiency in intimidation.
