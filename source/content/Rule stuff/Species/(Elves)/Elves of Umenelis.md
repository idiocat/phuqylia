**Demonic Affinity**. You have advantage against fear.

**Demonic features**. You have ashen skin/hair, burning eyes, etc.

**Inner Fire**. You can add your spellcasting ability mod. to persuasion and deception checks.

**Spread the Fire**. You can freely cast Absorb Element~~s~~, but only for fire damage. When Absorb Element~~s~~ is cast in this way, you can apply bonus damage to spells cast with Burn the World feature. You're immune to magical fire caused by you and all non-magical fire.

**Burn the World**. You learn Produce Flame cantrip. At lvl 3 you learn Burning Hands, you can cast this spell without expending a spellslot {prof.} times per long rest. If you're holding fire from the Produce Flame cantrip, when casting Burning Hands with this feature, you can hurl that fire as part of the casting.
