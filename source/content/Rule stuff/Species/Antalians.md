**Creature Type**: Humanoid (Insectoid)
**Size**: Medium (about 4-4.5 feet tall)
**Age expectancy**: 15 years (imago phase)
**Speed**: 30 feet

**Hive Specialization**. The hive knows what it needs.
* *Fighter*. +2 to str and dex, -1 to others; base dc is 13 + dex (while not wearing armour), powerful mandibles (1d6 bludg., +5 to dc when grappling and to end your grapple, also the grapple isn't cancelled if you're unconscious or dead).
* *Worker*. +2 to con and wis, -1 to others; 2 tool prof, also you can add your wis mod to your nature checks as well as one other non-wis skill of your choice.
* *Thinker*. +2 to int and cha, -1 to others; 2 kit prof and 2 languages, also you can add you cha or int mod to 2 wis skills of your choice.

Also, the maximum of your chosen abilities is increased by 4, while the maximum of others is decreased by 4. The default maximum of your ability scores is still considered 20 for the purpose of features that affect the maximum values.

**Secondary Arms**. You have two slightly smaller secondary arms below your primary pair of arms. The secondary arms can manipulate an object, open or close a door or container, pick up or set down a Tiny object, or wield a weapon that has the light property.

**Pheromone communication**. You gain advantage on perception checks related to smell. Also, you can communicate with other antalians non-verbally or leave/read pheromone-encoded messages.
