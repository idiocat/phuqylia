* **Size**: Medium (about 4-5.5 ft tall)
* **Speed**: 30 ft (walk, climb)

**Darkvision**. You have darkvision of 60 ft.

**Feline Agility**. You can use dexterity instead of strength to calculate jump distance or to make an unarmed strike. Any weapon that doesn't have heavy or two-handed property is considered to be finesse for you.

**Uncatchable**. You have proficiency in the Perception and Stealth skills.

**Apex Predator**. YWhen you reach lvl 3, you know Feline Grace and Locate Animals or Plants spells. You can cast any of them without expending a spellslot {prof.} times per long rest. While under the effect of Feline Grace cast with this feature, you can add your {prof.} to Perception and Stealth skill checks.
