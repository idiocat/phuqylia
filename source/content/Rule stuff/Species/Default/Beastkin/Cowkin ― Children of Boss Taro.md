* **Size**: Medium (about 5.5-7 ft tall)
* **Speed**: 30 ft

**Horns**. You're naturally horny. When you attack with your horns, you deal 1d6 + your str. mod piercing damage. Your horns have Push and Slow masteries.

**Goring Rush**. If you spend your action doing anything else than attack action, you can attack with your horns as a bonus action. Also, when you move at least 10 ft, your horns deal additional d6 damage on the next attack.

**Big Bulk**. Your AC is 12 + dex mod. You can use con instead of dex for any AC calculations.

**Labyrinthine Recall**. You always know which direction is north, and you have advantage on any survival check you make to navigate or track.
