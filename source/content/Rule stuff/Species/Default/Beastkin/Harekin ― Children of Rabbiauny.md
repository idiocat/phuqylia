- **Size**: Medium (about 4-5.5 ft tall)
- **Speed**: 35 ft

**Darkvision**. You have darkvision of 60 ft.

**Hare-Trigger**. You can add your {prof.} to initiative.

**Hareling Training**. You have prof. with longbows and pikes and you learn 1 mastery. When you finish a long rest, you can switch pike prof. to a prof. with a diff. weapon that has Reach property, you can also switch the mastery from this feature, but it has to apply to a weapon from this feature.

**Harewayman**. When you have disadvantage on attack related to vision (e.g., due to darkness or a spell effect like Blur), if there're no enemies around you, you can use your reaction to prick up your ears and make a perception check against DC {10 + target's stealth}, on a success you ignore that disadvantage against the target until the end of your turn.

**Hare Today, Gone Tomorrow**. When you take dash action, you can also take dodge action {prof.} times per long rest.
