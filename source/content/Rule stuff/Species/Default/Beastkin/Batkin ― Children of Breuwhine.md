- **Size**: Small (about 2-3 ft tall)
- **Speed**: 25 ft (walk, climb, fly)

**Blindsight**. You have blindsight of 20/60 ft*, that relies on hearing. You're blind otherwise.

**Echolocation**. Once per short rest, you can perform a 10 minute ritual to map the surrounding around you, you learn the passages stretching up to 500 ft away and you also learn if there're creatures.

**Сhiropteran**. You have flying speed as long as your hands are empty or only holding a tiny object or a light weapon (can't use them tho) and you're not wearing heavy armour. Also, you can use your feet as hands. 

**Unwinking Sentry**. You get +5 to passive perception and passive investigation. Also, your passive perception isn't reduced while sleeping.

**Weaponized Infrasound**. You know Vicious Mockery cantrip, when you cast it with this feature it deals thunder damage and has casting time of a bonus action.


\* long range: disadvantage on attack rolls and perception checks.
