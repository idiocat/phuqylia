* **Size**: Medium (about 5.5-7 ft tall)
* **Speed**: 30 ft

**Calm Monk**. You have advantage on saving throws against being charmed or frightened.

**Big Bulk**. Your AC is 12 + dex mod. You can use con instead of dex for any AC calculations.

**Keen Trunk**. You can grasp things with your trunk, and you can use it as a snorkel. It has a reach of 5 feet, and it can lift a number of pounds equal to five times your Strength score. You can use it to do the following simple tasks: lift, drop, hold, push, or pull an object or a creature; open or close a door or a container; make an unarmed strike. Also, you have advantage on checks that involve smell.

**Spread Funk**. You're prof. in insight. When you make a total of {7 - prof.} successful insight checks against people, you gain advantage on persuasion checks until the end of the long rest.
