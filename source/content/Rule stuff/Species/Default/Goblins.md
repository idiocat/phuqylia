**Creature Type**: Humanoid (Default)
**Size**: Small (about 3-4 ft tall)
**Life expectancy**: 50 years
**Speed**: 30 ft

**Goblin Cunning**. Whenever you fail a skill or a tool check you're prof. at, you can note the ability used fort that check. You can keep noted up to {int mod.} (min = 1) fails for redemption. You can then give yourself an advantage on a skill or a tool check you're prof. at that uses the noted ability (one advantage per a fail). Also, you know an additional language and you learn languages twice as fast.

**Goblin Nimbleness**. You can take Disengage or Hide action as a bonus action on each of your turns. Also, when you damage a creature with an attack or a spell and the creature's size is larger than yours, you can cause the attack or spell to deal extra damage to the creature equal to {prof.}  {prof.} times per long rest.
