**Fey Affinity**. You have advantage against charm.

**Fey features**. You have wooden horns, leaves in your hair or bark-like skin, etc. (at least one).

**Nature's Call**. Your Speed increases to 35 feet. You also know the Druidcraft cantrip. You learn Longstrider at level 3, and Pass Without Trace at level 5, you can cast each of these spells once without expending a spellslot.

**Extensive Understanding**. You have proficiency in the Insight, Perception or Survival skill.
