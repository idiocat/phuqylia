**Celestial Affinity**. Instead of rolling for the amount of hp healed, apply the maximum possible. This can be used once per long rest, as a reaction, on a creature that is receiving healing and that you can see within 30 feet.

**Celestial features**. You have small angelic wings (at least 2, usually on head). You may have more hair over your body.

**Winging it**. You can freely cast Jump, Longstrider and Feather Fall. When you cast these spells using this feature you can only target yourself and you can only have one of the spells active at a time. 

**Wanderer's Tools**. You know the Gust cantrip. You learn Distort Value at level 3, and Tongues at level 5, you can cast each of these spells once without expending a spellslot.
