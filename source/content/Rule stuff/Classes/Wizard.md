**Planar Prowess**. As a bonus action, you can tap into a leylane. Roll a d6, choose either: receive a spellcasting related bonus ([[Magic Bullshit]]) or, for the next minute, whenever you cast a spell that deals damage of one of the available types, you can add a die to one damage roll of that spell. Starting at lvl 7, the additional damage die can be of different type among the available ones. Starting at lvl 13, add 2 dice. Starting at lvl 17, you can always choose acid damage.
You can use this feature {your int mod} times per long rest.

| Die | Domain     | Damage Types                                                   |
| --- | ---------- | -------------------------------------------------------------- |
| 1   | Fey        | Piercing, Thunder, Poison, Force, Psychic                      |
| 2   | Celestial  | Slashing, Lightning, Thunder, Radiant                          |
| 4   | Primordial | Bludgeoning, Piercing, Slashing, Cold, Fire, Lightning, Poison |
| 3   | Aberrant   | Acid, Psychic, Radiant, Necrotic, Cold                         |
| 5   | Demonic    | Bludgeoning, Fire, Force, Necrotic                             |
| 6   | Fey        | Piercing, Thunder, Poison, Force, Psychic                      |

**Language of Power**. Your extensive study of magic runes gives you adv. on spellcasting checks made to modify spells. Also once per short rest you can give yourself advantage on a check made to counter or dispel a spell.

Also, you [[Trance]] to restore your spellslots.
