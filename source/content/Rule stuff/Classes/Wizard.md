**Planar Prowess**. As a bonus action, you can tap into a Plane. Roll a d6, on 5 or 6 you receive a spellcasting related bonus ([[Magic Bullshit]]). Otherwise, for the next minute, whenever you cast a spell that deals damage of one of the available types, you can add a die to one damage roll of that spell. Starting at lvl 7, the additional damage die can be of different type among the available ones. Starting at lvl 13, add 2 dice. Starting at lvl 17, on 5 and 6 you add 2 dice of acid damage.
You can use this feature {your int mod} times per long rest.

| Die | Plane     | Damage Types                              |
| --- | --------- | ----------------------------------------- |
| 1   | Fey       | Piercing, Thunder, Poison, Psychic, Force |
| 2   | Celestial | Slashing, Lightning, Thunder, Radiant     |
| 3   | Demonic   | Bludgeoning, Fire, Force, Necrotic        |
| 4   | Aberrant  | Acid, Psychic, Radiant, Necrotic, Cold    |

**Language of Power**. Your extensive study of magic runes gives you adv. on spellcasting checks made to modify spells. Also once per short rest you can give yourself advantage on a check made to counter or dispel a spell.

Also, you [[Trance]] to restore your spellslots.
