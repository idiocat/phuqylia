**Primordial Blessing**. While raging, if you fail a skill check, you can instead get the minimum required to succeed. This only applies to skills you're not prof. in. This can only be used once per rage.

**Primal Presence**. While raging, you can spend a bonus action to avoid adv. on attacks against you due to being surrounded until the start of your next turn. This can only be used once per rage.

**Primal Rage**. While raging, you can spend an action to rush into ultimate raging assault. This turn when taking attack action, you can make {max of rage charges + exhaustion lvl} attacks. At the end of your turn, you end your rage and gain a lvl of exhaustion. This can only be used once per rage. Also, while raging your exhaustion is considered one lvl lower.
