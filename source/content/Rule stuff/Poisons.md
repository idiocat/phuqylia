Poisoned condition is now called Weakened.
\[to avoid confusion]

When poison is applied to a weapon, the weapon receives a few potency charges, each attack expends one and applies it to the target. Amount of charges and effect depend on poison: e.g., some will get stronger with more potency charges applied, others will require a certain amount of charges applied before having any effect. When a potency charge is applied and at the end of its turn(s), the target makes a constitution save, on success one potency charge is removed.

poison dc = 8 + prof. + int mod.

caustic poison (4). deals 1 damage per potency at the start of the turn. this damage is non-lethal.
debilitating poison (3). applies weakened.
